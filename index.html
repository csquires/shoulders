About

Shoulders brings scientific journal citations to life by creating a web of articles. Each article is a node in the graph connected to papers that it has cited and papers that have cited it.

Developed by Woody Ahern, William Caruso, Taylor Herr, and Chandler Squires @ HackMIT 2015
